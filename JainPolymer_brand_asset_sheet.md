## 3. Typography (suggested, matching the print feel)
- Headings: a strong condensed/italic sans (the brochures use a bold italic for "JAIN POLYMER CO."). Web equivalent: **Oswald**, **Archivo**, or **Saira Condensed**.
- Body: a clean humanist sans — **Inter**, **Source Sans**, or **Roboto**.

## 5. Company / about copy
- Promoted in **1991** by Shri S.K. Jain (proprietor); long experience in cable manufacturing.
- Manufactures a wide range of PTFE/TEFLON-based products.
- Privately held; products have a specific role in the effective working of larger systems.
- Quality conforms to **JSS and MIL standards**; "no compromise on quality at any cost."
- Capable of developing custom products to client/customer requirements.
